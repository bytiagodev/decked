// Our story

We grew up
on the boardwalk.

DECKED started somewhere between the skatepark and the sand, where mornings begin on the boardwalk and end chasing the sunset. We were tired of boards that looked great in a photo and snapped a month later, and gear priced like it was made of gold instead of maple.

So we built our own. Decks made to last, wheels ready for long days on the boardwalk, and clothes comfortable enough to wear long after the session ends. Everything is designed to be used, not just looked at.
